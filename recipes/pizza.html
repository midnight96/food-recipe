Pizza

Description

Pizza is a globally popular dish of Italian origin, essentially a flattened disk of bread dough topped with a savory mixture, often including tomatoes and cheese, and then baked.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Toppings:

  • ½ cup tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for brushing)
  • Optional: Fresh basil, oregano, red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Make the dough: In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place it in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  4. Shape the dough: Once risen, punch down the dough and place it on a lightly floured surface. Roll or stretch it out into your desired pizza shape (e.g., a 12-inch circle).
  5. Add toppings: Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper. Brush the edges with olive oil. Spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake the pizza: Carefully slide the pizza (on the parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone or a baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
