Maggi

Description

Maggi is best known for its instant noodles, particularly the "2-Minute Noodles" variety. They also offer various flavors and healthier alternatives like Atta Noodles and Oats Noodles

Ingredients

  • maggi packet
  • water

Optional ingredients

  • cheese
  • chopped vegetables/meat
  • oil/butter
  • sauces
  • spices

    • Steps

    1. Boil water: Bring 1 cup (or around 210 ml) of water to a boil in a pan.
    2. Add noodles and seasoning: Once the water is boiling, add the Maggi noodles (you can break them into pieces if you prefer) and the Tastemaker seasoning from the packet to the boiling water.
    3. Cook for about 2 minutes while stirring occasionally, or until the noodles are soft and cooked through. Do not drain any remaining water.
    4. Let the noodles sit for about a minute after cooking before serving them hot
