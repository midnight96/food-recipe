Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of wide, flat pasta, flavorful sauces, and melted cheese. It's a popular comfort food

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese
  3. Bake for 45 minutes
